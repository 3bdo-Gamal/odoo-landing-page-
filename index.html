Project Overview

What is Odoonova?

Odoonova is a graduation project built to demonstrate how Artificial Intelligence can enhance modern ERP systems. It extends Odoo with an AI assistant, predictive forecasting, interactive dashboards, and automated reporting — showing, in practice, how businesses can move from raw data to confident decisions.

Intelligent Forecasting

Predicts trends from historical business data.

Smart Business Insights

Ask questions and get instant, data-backed answers.

Workflow Automation

Reduces repetitive manual operational tasks.

About The Project

Academic Purpose & Vision

Purpose

To demonstrate, through a working system, how AI can be integrated into an open-source ERP to genuinely improve daily business operations.

Vision

A future where small and medium businesses have access to intelligent, forecast-driven ERP tools without needing a dedicated data science team.

Academic Value

Applies concepts from ERP systems, machine learning, and full-stack development within a single, coherent graduation project.

Future Improvements

Planned directions include deeper forecasting models, expanded automation modules, and broader Odoo module coverage.
